SAVER - a new way to save

Buy too many coffees? Amazon parcels keep ariving at your door? Need help to save?

Instead of buying that next coffee, open the Saver app, click a button and transfer money directly into you savings account, or to a charity*

We aim to make saving easy!

Save more - Spend less

We challenge you to actively save for furtue goals by not indulging now

Uses
  • Break bad habits and create new better ones
  • Actively save towards a goal
  • Teach children how to save
  • Easily donate to a range of charities
Set up Quickly
  • Login
  • Add a “Saver”
  • Click to save

Moving into the future

*The app is currently unfinished, and the app you can download is a proof of concept. There is no transfer functionality, so the app currently works as a tracker for your savings. We have published this app to gain feedback that will help to quickly iterate new and better solutions. So please test, send in feedback and share with your friends. We are looking to raise a small amount of money to provide the full "Saver" experience, by implementing all of the features below.

Free Transfer
This application is designed to help you to save money. We believe this should be quick and simple, as well as costing the user nothing to transfer funds
Charities
Imagine if instead of eating out, you could click a button and send that money directly to a charity. And imagine if that charity could tell you exactly what that money has gone towards. E.g. if you donate £15 to "X" charity, 10 children would get a meal
Reward System
A reward system would keep you coming back to the app, and improve your saving habits! Rewards could be in the form of vouchers, savings bonuses, donations to charity
Improve your personal finance
Produce regular articles, how-to guides, and additional features to help you to improve your personal finance, so you can spend your money on the important things
